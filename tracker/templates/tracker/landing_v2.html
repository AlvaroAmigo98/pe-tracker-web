How it works

Simple by design

01
Automated weekly scrape
Every Monday, our pipeline visits 100+ PE firm team pages and captures the full roster — names, titles, seniorities, and functions.
02
Change detection
Each new snapshot is compared to last week's. New names, missing names, and title changes are automatically classified as hires, leavers, or promotions.
03
Signals surface
Events are tagged with seniority, function, and region — making filtering instant. Cascade patterns and notable moves are flagged automatically.
04
You stay ahead
Log in Monday morning and review every market move before anyone else. No manual LinkedIn trawls. No missed signals.
