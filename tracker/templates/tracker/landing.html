How It Works
Simple by design

Fully automated intelligence — no manual work, no data entry.

01
Automated weekly scrape
Every Monday, a scheduled pipeline visits 100+ PE firm team pages and extracts the current roster — names, titles, and functions.
02
Change detection
The new snapshot is compared against last week's. Any new name, missing name, or title change is automatically classified as a hire, leaver, or promotion.
03
Signals surface
Events are tagged with seniority level, function (Buyout, Infrastructure, Advisory), and region — making filtering and search instant.
04
You stay ahead
Log in on Monday morning and review what changed across the entire market. No more manual LinkedIn trawls or missed signals.
